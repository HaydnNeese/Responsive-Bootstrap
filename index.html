About Me

Haydn is a student at the KU Coding Bootcamp, learning to be a profession web developer. He is based out of Lawrence, Kansas; orginally from Oklahoma. He has a passion for learning new things and putting his creative mind to work in new and exciting ways. Ever since he was a child he had wanted a job working with computers that would allow him to create and design the things that he imagined in his mind and now he is finally pursuing his dream.


He graduated in 2015 from Ottawa University with a B.A. in Exercise Science and worked from 2015-2018 as a personal trainer in Lawrence, Kansas. After a few years, he discovered though he liked his career as a personal trainer he desired a more livable and reliable income for all his hard work. Thus, he decided to return to school to pursue the job as a computer programmer that he always dreamed of.


He has a willingness to learn and a desire to make a change wherever he goes. Given the chance, he believes that he will succeed and do great things for himself and the company that is willing to put his hard work to good use.
